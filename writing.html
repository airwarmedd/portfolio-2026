Newsletter

Personal Newsletter: Anecdotes from my life

I started this newsletter in 2021. I didn't really know what I was doing. I just wanted a space that wasn't about productivity or "value." Just a place to put thoughts. Over the years it's quietly received a lot of love, mostly from people who know me well, which is probably why it's so personal to me. It's not about trends or lessons or hot takes. It's about what I see in my everyday life. What I feel. How I'm moving through my 20s, and now my late 20s.
